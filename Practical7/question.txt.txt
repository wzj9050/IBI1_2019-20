Q: How have new cases and total cases developed over time in Spain? 
A: The code starts from row 66.
The total cases and new cases were plain and nearly 0 from the beginning to 3.8.
The total cases and new cases began to increase on 3.8.
The growth rate of total cases was increasing until 3.27, when the new cases began to decrease.
The new cases grew with fluctuation steady and got a higher growth rate on 3.24 after a local peak on 3.22.
